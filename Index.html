Prep & Packing
Monsoon season
Early June = shoulder season. Rough surf, red-flag beach days, and some clubs may be closed. Embrace the rain — Goa in monsoon is actually magic.
⚠️
Confirm venues before arriving
Raeth, Masaya, and Dynamo Goa — check Instagram or call ahead. Monsoon closures are real.
📅
Flea market = Wednesdays only
Anjuna Flea Market runs Wednesdays only. Day 2 is a Friday — swapped for a village walk.
💸
Cash is king
Many beach shacks are cash-only. Keep ₹2–3K on you. ATMs run dry on weekends.
🛵
Scooter in monsoon
Wet roads = slippery, especially Vagator–Chapora hill bends. Slow down. Helmets on.
🌧️
Rain backup spots
Artjuna Café, Bodega, or a long lazy lunch. Showers usually pass in 30–45 min.
🏨
Check-in reality check
Hotels check in at noon. Arriving Day 1? Confirm early check-in or leave bags and head to brunch.
🏖️
Beach swimming
Red flag = no swimming. Monsoon surf is strong. Stick to shore and enjoy the vibe.
